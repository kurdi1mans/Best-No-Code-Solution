# Fibery
**Category:** Work management database/docs hybrid

- **Summary:** Flexible workspace combining relational databases with documents and whiteboards for product/knowledge teams.
- **Strengths:**
  - Highly customizable schema with relations, formulas, and bidirectional links across databases.
  - Combines structured data with docs/notes; good for research, product discovery, and planning.
  - Automations, rules, and API enable integrations; generous views and permissions.
- **Limitations:**
  - Smaller ecosystem and fewer turnkey templates vs. Airtable/ClickUp.
  - Performance and UX can vary with very large workspaces.
  - Public-facing app/site use cases are limited; primarily internal.
- **Best for:** Product/knowledge teams needing connected research, tasks, and databases in one workspace.
- **Pricing snapshot:** Free tier for small teams; paid adds users, automation limits, and advanced permissions.
- **Notable integrations:** API, webhooks, Zapier/Make, browser extensions, two-way sync with some tools.

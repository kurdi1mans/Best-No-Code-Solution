# Webflow
**Category:** Website & landing page builder

- **Summary:** Visual designer with CMS and hosting aimed at production-grade marketing sites without writing code.
- **Strengths:**
  - Pixel-level control, modern animations, responsive tools baked in.
  - CMS collections good for blogs, resource hubs, and simple catalogs.
  - Clean output, fast hosting/CDN, built-in SEO controls.
- **Limitations:**
  - Dynamic data limits; complex logic or authenticated apps require workarounds or add-ons.
  - Multi-language and content localization need third-party tools or duplication.
  - Pricing tied to traffic, CMS items, and seats can rise quickly at scale.
- **Best for:** Marketing sites, landing pages, and content-heavy sites where design quality matters.
- **Pricing snapshot:** Site plans start low for basic sites; CMS/business tiers add items and traffic; workspaces priced per seat.
- **Notable integrations:** Native CMS API, Zapier/Make, Memberstack/Outseta for auth/memberships, e-commerce beta, embeds.
